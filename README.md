# Stickerverse

**Stick to What Matters**

## About the Business

Stickerverse is a premium vinyl sticker company serving creatives, businesses, and individuals who demand quality that lasts. We specialize in both curated collections and custom printing services, delivering weather-resistant stickers with 3-5 year outdoor life expectancy.

### What We Offer

**Curated Collections**
- Tech & Code
- Vintage Vibes
- Nature & Wildlife
- Pop Culture
- Minimalist
- Typography

**Custom Orders**
Upload your design and receive professional-grade vinyl stickers printed on premium materials. We handle everything from artwork proofing to fast dispatch within 48 hours of approval.

**Quality Promise**
- Weather-resistant vinyl that won't fade
- 100% recyclable materials
- 30-day money-back guarantee
- Free Kenya shipping on custom orders past 20$

### Target Audience

- Creative professionals personalizing laptops and equipment
- Small businesses needing branded materials
- Event organizers and marketers
- Gift buyers seeking unique, lasting presents
- Anyone tired of cheap stickers that peel off in a week

### Business Model

- Direct-to-consumer e-commerce
- Custom B2B printing services
- Wholesale partnerships
- Volume discounts at 500+ units
- Starting from KES80 per sticker
